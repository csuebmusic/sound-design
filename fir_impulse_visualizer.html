Key insight: The impulse response of an FIR filter IS its coefficients. Feed in a single 1 (followed by zeros) and read off the output — you get exactly [1/N, 1/N, ..., 1/N] over N steps, then silence. Any LTI system with a finite impulse response can be modeled this way.
