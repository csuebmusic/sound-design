Every filter type is the same structure — different coefficients.
Feedforward (g, b1, b2) place zeros — frequencies where gain = 0.   Feedback (a1, a2) place poles — frequencies where gain peaks.
LPF, HPF, BPF, notch, peak, and shelving filters are all specific pole/zero configurations of the BiQuad.
